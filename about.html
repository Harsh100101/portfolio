Who I am

About me

A final-year CSE (AI) student who builds things that actually get used.

I'm Harsh Sorathiya, a React Native and frontend developer based in Gujarat, India. I'm currently in my final year of B.Tech in Computer Science (AI specialisation) at Parul University, Vadodara — and I completed a React Native internship from January to April 2026, shipping real features on a live production product.

My focus is on mobile and web apps that feel right — fast to load, intuitive to use, clean to maintain. I've been writing React Native since early 2026, and I've worked across the stack with React.js, Redux Toolkit, Node.js, Flask, PostgreSQL, and Supabase.

I got into AI and recommendation systems through my final-year research — I published a paper in IJSET in October 2025 on personalised recommendation pipelines, then built that research into a real full-stack platform (The Next Read), which I load-tested at 200 concurrent users with JMeter.

In 2024, I led my team in a healthcare AI hackathon at Parul University — handling architecture, coordination, and full-stack delivery within the hackathon timeline. It taught me how to build fast under real constraints.

Right now I'm looking for freelance projects and full-time roles in React Native and frontend development — Gujarat-first, but open to Pune, Noida, and remote-friendly teams across India and internationally.
